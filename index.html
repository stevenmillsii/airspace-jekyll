About Us

Our team has more than 40 years of regional and national online experience serving clients in the transportation, medical, banking, apparel, and communication industries.

Kevin S. Cooke, our tireless leader, began his career with a decade of service at Raven Knob Scout Reservation (eventually becoming the Program Director there) before exploring the nation as a fly fishing guide and professional driver with Toyota Trail Teams. He now utilizes his extensive experience as a guide and instructor to demystify the online space for clients and help them set and achieve business goals. Kevin resides in Charlotte, NC, with his wife Kaily and their son Whittman.
